SPCA Montreal - Chat Widget Example

Welcome to the SPCA Montreal website. This page demonstrates the AI chat widget.

About SPCA Montreal

The SPCA Montreal is dedicated to animal welfare and finding loving homes for animals in need.

We have dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, and small animals available for adoption.

How to Use the Chat Widget

  1. Click the blue chat button in the bottom-right corner
  2. Ask questions about our animals, services, or adoption process
  3. The AI assistant will provide helpful information

Example Questions

  • What dogs are available for adoption?
  • What are the adoption fees?
  • What are your opening hours?
  • How can I adopt a pet?
